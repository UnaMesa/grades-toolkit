Instructions:

This process is based on the assumption that a student should remain in their current school* unless the considerations on the following pages indicate that a change of school placement is in the child’s best interest.

  • This form is to be filled out at the child’s date of placement and each time there is a change in living arrangement.

  • Individuals with insight into the child’s educational best interest are encouraged to participate in a BID meeting or conversation.

  • You may use relevant information from recent meetings (i.e., EST, CSP, IEP or 504).


*Current school is defined as the school the child is attending prior to a change in living arrangement.
